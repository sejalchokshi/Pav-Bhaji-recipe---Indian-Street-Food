Pav-Bhaji Recipe

Learn how to make this delicious Indian street food step by step!

Ingredients

  • 4 Pav bread rolls
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup mashed boiled potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped capsicum
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon Pav Bhaji masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon wedges and fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Mix in capsicum, peas, and mashed potatoes. Stir well.
  4. Add Pav Bhaji masala, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Mash the mixture while cooking to achieve a smooth consistency.
  6. Toast Pav bread with butter on a griddle until golden brown.
  7. Serve the Bhaji hot, garnished with coriander and a wedge of lemon, alongside toasted Pav.
